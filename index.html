The Proud Heritage of the Ekhoade Family

The Ekhoade Family stands as a beacon of honor, unity, and integrity. Across generations, the family has upheld a standard of dignity and close-knit bonds, ensuring that every member reflects these foundational values.

As a vital branch of the Iviuefo kindred of Iviukwe, located in the heart of the Uwanno Kingdom (Etsako East, Edo State), the Ekhoade lineage continues to nurture a legacy of excellence. Our roots are deeply intertwined with the history of Agenebode, thriving from its 16th-century origins into a modern era of cultural pride and communal strength.
